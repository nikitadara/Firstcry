 Feeding

FirstCry recognizes the importance of
breastfeeding & at this may be a new
experiance for you.We suggest the following to
help make this special bonding-time with baby
more comfortable & enjoyable:

    • Feeding cloak to help ease any discomfort
      while feeding baby in public
    • For all the cleaning up after feeding nursing wipes
      & pads would be of great use
    • Breast pumps to help pump out the milk
    • Breast-milk storage bottles to store milk for later use
    • Nipple protectors & formers to help baby
      latch on
    • Protect yourself from any infection with nipple creams
    • Provide comfort while feeding with nursing pillow
    • Nursing bras and tops to ease feeding
    • Baby food
