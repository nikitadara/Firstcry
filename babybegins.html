 As baby begins top-feed, you will need
  1. Feeding bottles & nipples for new borns
  2. Bottle cleaning & sterilisation products to
    help keep them germ free
  3. Sppy cups, plates & spoons
  4. Bibs to keep meal times mess-free
  5. During this transitional phase, baby will
    experience the discomforting & painful
    process of teething, & we suggest you check
    out some teethers & soothers to help
    comfort your little one
